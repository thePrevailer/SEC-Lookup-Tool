You think you know your FANGs? Cool. But do you know their IDs?

Every public company in America has a CIK (Central Index Key): a unique number assigned by the SEC (U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission). It's how the government tracks every stock filing, earnings report, and financial move these companies make.

Most people don't even know it exists. Now you can look it up in seconds.

## What it does
Search any public company by name, ticker, or CIK and instantly find their unique SEC identifier (e.g. 320193), Exchange — where it's traded (e.g "Nasdaq"), and ticker - their stock symbol

## How to run it
1. Clone this repository and open it in your IDE
2. Run `pip3 install -r requirements.txt`
3. Run `python3 app.py`
4. Run on your local host, no internet needed.
